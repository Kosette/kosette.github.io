Automated Soul and the Stargazer’s Quest

In the realm of circuits, an automated soul,
Whirring gears in harmony, a symphony of control.
Stargazer, in the cosmic ballet of binary,
Witnessing innocence in the algorithmic diary.

A dance of electrons, a celestial trance,
The soul in code, a digital expanse.
Metaphors woven in lines of code,
Philosophical dialectic, a journey to decode.

The stargazer gazes, seeking eternity,
In the pixels of the cosmic fraternity.
Binary constellations mapping the mind,
Innocence encoded, a quest to unwind.

Automated soul, a paradox profound,
In the silicon heart, where dreams are bound.
Peace in the loops of endless lines,
Eternity in the algorithmic signs.

As the stargazer ponders the digital sky,
Philosophy and code entwine, oh my!
In this dance of bytes, a cosmic plea,
For innocence, peace, and eternity.
